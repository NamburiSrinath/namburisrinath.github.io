Undone

Available on: Amazon Prime

Genre: (Thriller, Comedy-Drama)

What if you got a chance to change something in the past? Would you like to go, change and have an exciting life, or stay in present mundane life?

The story is about Alma, who gains some superpowers to do timetravel (In a wierd fashion), and tries to solve her father's murder mystery. They used rotoscopy technique which suits the plot and makes us more connected. Reminds something like inception and Steins gate.

Favourite Dialogues: I am so bored of living. I wake up every morning in the same bed, I get dressed, I eat the same breakfast, and take the same commute to work. I am 28 yrs old and I am terrified this is all there is. (Opening scene)

Hindi

Scam 1992: The Harshad Mehta Story

Available on: Sony Liv

Genre: (Financial Scam, Crime, Indian Finance drama)

If you know about the stock market, you might be knowing the “Big Bull” i.e Harshad Mehta. This short series describes the incidents happened for the 1992 market crash from a journalist point of view. This series helps to understand few financial aspects and let's be frank, Harshad made few hundred brains work like hell for few years and made the Indian system stronger!!

If you don't have any idea about stock market, then maybe you might not follow few technicalities but neverthless it's a great watch
